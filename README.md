# E-Commerce-Sales-Profit-Analysis-Dashboard

## 📊 Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of e-commerce sales performance, including revenue, profit, orders, and customer trends. It enables data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations and detailed insights across regions, categories, and products.

## 🎯 Key Insights

- 📈 Total Sales reached 33.29M, growing by 4.2% YoY, indicating steady business expansion  
- 💰 Profit increased to 10.76M (+3.2%), but growth is slower than sales, signaling margin pressure  
- 📊 Profit Margin declined to 32.33% (-0.9%), highlighting rising costs or increased discounting impact  
- 🛒 Orders grew by 2.1% (5,053 orders), while quantity increased by 3.0% (15K units), indicating slightly higher order size  
- 🎯 Discount increased by 0.8% (75K), which may have supported sales growth but negatively impacted margins  
- ⚖️ Overall performance shows healthy growth, but profitability efficiency is slightly declining, requiring cost and pricing optimization
